CTP : Connected Samurai - The Customer Journey

Created by Lakshminarayanan Mukundagiri (TMNA) on May 07, 2018

The Product owner is both responsible and accountable for the successful delivery of the products and services rendered by the Scrum team for the overall CT Family. The user/ customer for the App services offered are Toyota & Lexus Owners who are excited to use effective technology for their vehicles that ensures - Ease of use, Safety & Security.

Our PO - Rock Mutchler


Who is Our Consumer

  • Some one who is excited about technology use - Tech Savy
  • Power user who is safety conscious at all times
  • Everything is connected via hand held device. this to begin is the connected experience to perform all activities through the use of technology in internal/ external environments

 

How Does the Customer Finds Us

  • They Purchase a Vehicle - Toyota/ Lexus
  • They saw a commercial or heard one for use and liked it
  • Word of mouth in all possible means 

 

What are their Pain Points

  • Lack of reliable services - In and Out functions
  • Unstable services offered for the premium paid for
  • Quite frankly the services are also not cost effective on a comparative competitor scale
  • Ease of learning to use services offered (intuitively is the key - No one teaches anyone how to use mobile Apps)

 

What excites the Customer

  • Tell them what is new and how can they employ it for use
  • New features and functions which can be viewed and applied for use in their daily life
  • Communicate passionately innovation that excites the customer for the products/ services chosen with Toyota/ Lexus

 

What irks the Customer

  • Outages and Malfunction when they need the service the most - This has severe service impacts and bad reviews 
  • Complicated user experience which diminishes the intent of the service/ function

 

Why Toyota for customers - why not something else - What is our Edge?

  • We strive to satisfy the customer with technology in all our human excellence
  • Of course the looks - we pride ourselves in what we make be it Vehicle or Vehicle services - we improve continuously
  • Simplify Life through ease of use 
  • Endurance and Quality to remain the bench mark for what ever we have to offer to the customer
  • While we follow the customer, we also are excited to show the customer new experiences
