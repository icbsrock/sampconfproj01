Repeat/Avoid is a simple and effective data gathering activity. It is is based on  two very specific questions: What to repeat? What to avoid?
The Activity:
Please add your notes accordingly:

  • What should we REPEAT ?
  • What should we AVOID ?



I recommend pre defining categories for the notes.
The picture below shows this activity results. The following are the pre-defined categories:   People / Process / Technology / Scope
