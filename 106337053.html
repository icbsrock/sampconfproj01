CTP : Product Owner Info - Rock Mutchler (The Yoda)

Created by Vijay Vogirala (TMNA), last modified by Lakshminarayanan Mukundagiri (TMNA) on Apr 18, 2018

Product Owner Availability

100 % and at all times available for the Team. The priorities for the PO is unbiasly divided for a good 1/2 time for the team to guide/ Direct and clarify priorities & undivided time to engage with customers to identify key pain areas to solve 

Does the PO attend Daily Scrum meetings?

  1. As much when possible to hear from the team what their progress has been towards their Sprint
  2. Unavailable if OOO, Sick or Engaged with a Customer Discussion of priority to the team

What to do when the PO is not available: 

  1. Reach his/ her Cell - unless there is an explicit OOO/ Sick notice
  2. Use Skype or email channels if remote to seek information
  3. Talk to respective Leads for immediate guidance
  4. Finally talk to CPO (Siva) if matter needs urgent attention

For Sprint planning or sprint review: PO Required for Both

Sprint Review is PO Facilitated for the team & the Customers

Sprint Planning is the Negotiation discussion between PO's Customer priorities and Team's comfort of commitment in incremental fashion

For a meeting:

Certainly PO needed for Planning, Refinement and Review - In Person

For a question:

Check with PO in person. It is completely OK to receive work items from other teams just ensure the visibility is open for the PO

For an extended period of time? (vacation, etc.)

The PO at this time would have clearly indicated a Road map with definitive milestones the team to align commitment for.

Any leave of Absence or Vacation the team and the Scrum Master will shoulder the PO's priorities in line with their expectation for the customers
