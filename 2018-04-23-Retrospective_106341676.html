General Behavior Activity

 

The General behavior activity fosters a team conversation about behavior. It is specially useful when forming new teams. The results of this activity serve as an ongoing guide for acceptable and unacceptable team behavior.

Running the activity:
 
1.      Separate the canvas in two separate areas with the following titles
  • Wonderful behavior
  • Unacceptable behavior
  • Acceptable Behavior
2.   Ask the participants to write on post-its (and place at the canvas) examples of wonderful behavior and unacceptable behavior.
3.   Group conversation about the notes.
Wonderful Behavior :


a) Open Communication - The team is open to communicate on availability, access, asking for help, willingness to train
b) Every one has a voice and an opportunity to share healthy opinion - The freedom to agree/ Disagree
c) Team feels safe and open to welcome health debates and engage in challenging each other

Unacceptable Behavior:


a) Meetings can be optimized to allow more time to work (This is any meetings anything outside discussions)
b) Ad hoc sidelining of team members for Other team priorities - This crowds the team - 
c) Backlog to be T-Shirt sized - PO has offered to produce this and T Shirt size so that we can have a healthy release burn down visualized
d) We are uncovering process so there is going to be bump in velocity
Acceptable Behavior: 



a) Scope Changes - Team's ability to welcome changes - it is not the intent to interrupt but negotiation to Change (6/8)
b) Team Collaboration - Pairing for work efforts; Cross exchanging ideas
C) Scrum Events - Team is always participate of all the Scrum events  (Retro, Refinement, Demo, Planning, Stand up)
d) Do not wait until the last day to Close your work items and focus one work item at a time
