CTP : Definition of - READY & DONE

Created by Lakshminarayanan Mukundagiri (TMNA) on May 07, 2018

For the connected Samurai's the following are principle guidelines to ensure the work effort adheres to INVEST Stories that are small and measurable. The focus for the committment is a mutual agreement of what is ready to what gets done

Definition of Ready


1. Agreed Upon Acceptance criteria that are clear, unambiguous and defined

2. No outstanding decision is a dependency for the work effort ready to increment

3. There is no skill GAPS outside of the team to reach out for help

4. Access requirements with personnel and systems are in place for the work effort

5. Sprint GOAL is clear per the planned/ agreed upon items

6. Specific info to present DEMO and agreed upon artifacts for the increment

7. Meets Coding Standards defined, Releasable and achieves the expected outcome of the story

8. Peer Review and Unit test performed for every increment of code performed - Employ automation testing as much possible

9. Any increment of code does not involve Technical DEBT which impacts the quality of the code/ function delivered
