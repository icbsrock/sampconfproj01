Retrospective - Lessons learned quadrants (planning vs success)


This activity is great for seeking lessons learned after a big event.

Setting up the activity:

1. Prepare and explain the Lessons Learned canvas.

Running the activity:

Please share your notes for the given context.

Following the color code, create your notes and place them accordingly:

The bigger the success, the higher you should place your note (failures goes to the lower part of the canvas).

If you consider your note is about something highly planned, then place it on the very left; otherwise you should place it on the right side of the canvas.

2. Decide upon post it colors (each color should cover a given category/area. e.g resources, technology, schedule)
3. Ask participants to write their notes
4. Group conversation
