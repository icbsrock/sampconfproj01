CTP : Samurai - Team Working Agreements

Created by Lakshminarayanan Mukundagiri (TMNA) on May 07, 2018

We the connected Samurai have derived the following working agreements in line with the Scrum values which we uphold and adhere to.

Â 

Scrum ValueTeam Agreements Echoed and agreed upon for Practice
Openness
  • Customer First - Do the Right Thing the Right way
  • Communication - Communicate everything and as much as possible Quickly
  • Lunch & Learns / Offsite time - Team building and knowing the fellow team members outside of the work life
  • Demo as a Team - Success of Failure stay together as one voice
Respect
  • Reward/ Compliment - Brag for what you want to show off - It's your effort you deserve the recognition
  • Respect Everyone - Not all 5 fingers the same, everyone has a purpose and a value inherent for growth
  • Ceremonies are not Optional - It's our meetings for the team and of the team, No excuses please
Courage
  • Challenge the Ideas - Debate and Argue intensely on every idea explored for most outcome
  • Don't be afraid of Failures - Failures are part of the journey - Glad to try and Fail than Not to
  • Own your decisions all the way - Freedom of work and choice comes with responsibility enjoy it wisely
Committment
  • Core Hours - 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM - we all agree this is necessary for one of us to support our priorities
  • WFH when needed - While remote is an option it should never be abused
  • Sworm and Huddle - Engage in focused brief sessions for objective as much as possible
  • Remain available - Assist fellow team members if one's own priorities are completed
  • Artifacts - No one tells any team member how to present their work - You constantly improve pursue your excellence and the only reference of measure is one's own self work
Focus
  • Status Quo - Do not be ever afraid to ask Questions - No such thing as Stupid Question
  • Artifacts - JIRA, Confluence, BB (Mark Down - Please) - Allow peer review as best practice to your work
  • No meeting Day - Wednesday of every week - There isn't any meetings for the team
  • Enjoy your work - Anything you enjoy gets your fullest attention - Deliver with care every increment
