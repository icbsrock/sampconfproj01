Retrospective - Open the Box

 

This activity foster innovation and challenges the current activities performed by the team.
Running the activity:
1. Start by reading the following quote

“The world as we have created it is a process of our thinking. It cannot be changed without changing our thinking.” ― Albert Einstein

2. Bring the participants attention to the box metaphor (If possible, bring a box with you).

3. Split the whiteboard or canvas in 3 areas. Draw the open box in the center.
4. Explain each of the areas:

Please add your notes to the areas accordingly:   

  • Which activities should
    be removed from it?


 

a. Being late or remote to any ceremonies
b. Over committing when its rush hour - feel empowered to say NO - Negotiate
c. We really need to encourage as a team to allow app teams to create their own release pipelines for PERF/ STG


  • Which activities should be added?


 
a. Any support and enhancements for on boarding services to higher environments for aiding App teams
b. Collective through put for Notification platform and Content management
c. Align priority work items for documenting the activities - Remember Scrum is about documenting what we do 


  • What to recycle?

 



a. Weekly demo and more customer feedback
b. Continues up-gradation and high level/new tool integration → Focus HA/DR Documentation
c. Identifying areas of integration that are necessary for all environments - QA, PERF, STG



Contributor - Connected Samurai



Actions

  • Document work items for which we set up on platform/ pipeline activities
  • Stay close and communicate clearly to specifics internally and externally
