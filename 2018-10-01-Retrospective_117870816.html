Retrospective - Retrospective - Repeat – Avoid

 
Repeat/Avoid is a simple and effective data gathering activity. It is is based on  two very specific questions: What to repeat? What to avoid?


Running the activity:
Please add your notes accordingly:

  • What should we repeat? Gather all inputs that we love to do, continue more of this - remember to echo things within team's control
  • What should be avoided? Gather all inputs for things that should stop - remember to echo things within the team's control
